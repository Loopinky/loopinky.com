7. Cookies & Tracking

This website uses Google Analytics for traffic analysis. Google Analytics sets cookies to track visitor behavior. These cookies are only loaded after you explicitly accept them via our cookie consent banner, in compliance with Article 5(3) of the ePrivacy Directive.

If you refuse cookies, no tracking data is collected. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage.

We also use localStorage (not cookies) to store your cookie consent choice and popup display preference. This is strictly necessary for the website to function and does not require consent under the ePrivacy Directive.

Your personal data (name, email) is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed in section 5, strictly for the purposes described.

9. Data Retention

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, or until you request its deletion. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted.

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. The website uses HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
