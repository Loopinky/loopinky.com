Best Calligraphy Workbooks in 2026: Our Top Picks

February 2026 • 8 min read • By Loopinky

Whether you're a complete beginner looking to learn calligraphy or an intermediate artist wanting to refine your skills, the right practice workbook makes all the difference. We've reviewed the most popular calligraphy and lettering workbooks available on Amazon to help you find the perfect one for your needs.

What Makes a Great Calligraphy Workbook?

Before diving into our picks, here's what we look for in a quality calligraphy workbook:

  • Guided practice: step-by-step exercises that build skills progressively
  • Multiple styles: variety keeps practice interesting and develops versatility
  • Complete alphabets: full A-Z, numbers, punctuation and special characters
  • Paper quality: smooth pages that work well with brush pens and markers
  • Page count: enough practice space to truly develop muscle memory

Our Top Picks

1. June & Lucy Hand Lettering Workbooks

June & Lucy is one of the most well-known names in the hand lettering workbook space. Their books are beautifully designed with a modern, minimalist aesthetic that appeals to a wide audience. They focus primarily on modern calligraphy and brush lettering, making them an excellent starting point for beginners who want to learn flowing, feminine scripts.

Their workbooks typically include tracing guides, practice pages and inspirational quotes to letter. The design quality is consistently high, and the instructions are clear and beginner-friendly. If you're drawn to elegant, modern calligraphy styles, June & Lucy is a solid choice.

How to Choose the Right Workbook

The best calligraphy workbook depends on your goals:

  • For modern calligraphy: June & Lucy or Loopinky Beach Premium
  • For comics and bold lettering: Loopinky Comics Lettering Vol.1 or Vol.2
  • For creative projects: Amy Latta's books
  • For themed cursive practice: Loopinky Western Cowboy or Cowgirl
  • For maximum variety: consider getting 2-3 different workbooks to practice multiple styles
The best time to start practicing calligraphy is now. Pick one workbook, grab a brush pen, and start with the basics. You'll be amazed at your progress after just a few weeks of daily practice.
